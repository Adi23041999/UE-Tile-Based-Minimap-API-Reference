Show Zone

Broadcasts a message with the intention of showing a zone on the minimap (eg. highlight a specific neighbourhood on the map)
NOTE: listening objects may recieve multiple tags with each one having it's own color tag
Users should setup a system where they pick up the best color tag to decide on final zone color
